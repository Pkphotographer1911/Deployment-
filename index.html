üö¶ Deployment Steps

  1. Get WhatsApp Session:
    • Click "Get Session Website" to generate your QR session.
    • Scan the QR code using WhatsApp on your phone.
  2. Fork the Repository:
    • Click "Fork Repo" to create your own copy of the bot.
  3. Deploy the Bot:
    • Choose where to deploy: Heroku, Railway, or Render.
    • Click the respective button and follow the platform's instructions.
  4. Configure Environment Variables:
    • Set the SESSION_ID with the value from the session website.
  5. Star the Repository!
    • Support PK-XMD by starring on GitHub.
  6. Manual Setup:
    • To run locally, download the ZIP file, extract, and follow instructions in README.md.
